Pending Challenge: Osama Bin-Spection

About this challenge

Healthy bins mean less litter, the council owns and maintains over 300 litter bins, which need to be inspected and fixed on a regular basis.

You can help out by visiting the bins in your area , taking a photo and reporting any problems like bins being full, damaged or missing.

The challenge

Help reduce litter by helping the council keep your bins in good working order.

The Reward:

  • 5 points toward your Litter score
  • A shiny bin batch for your enviromate

How to Participate

To complete the challenge your team must:

  2. Take a photo of the bin and check its registration number
  3. Post the photo and answer 3 questions about the bin on the Council web site.
