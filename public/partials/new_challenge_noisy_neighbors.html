New Challenge: Noisy Neighbors

About this challenge

Noise from student houses is a concern for everybody

We have partnered with Town and Gown - a student/council liason group, to help reduce levels of late-night noise in student housholds

The challenge

Help reduce late-night noise in your area due to student housing by reporting student noise

The Reward:

  • 5 points toward your score

Bonus Points Tweet from your Neighbor's park

Suggest this challenge

How this challenge works

The council tracks noise complaints - we'll track your progress in reducing noise in your comunity and give you points depending on how well you perform.

Sign up with Town and Gown's Noise reduction program and report student noise issues.
